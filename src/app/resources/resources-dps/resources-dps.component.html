Warcraft Logs

Includes intricate details about a fight, similar to Details! or Recount but with more details.

WoW Analyzer

Breaks down information from Warcraft Logs and tells you where you have room for improvement.

WeakAuras

On-screen alerts, shapes, sounds, and more that give you a leg-up on fights.

Hero Damage

A website where you can see the latest World of Warcraft simulations results for every class.

Raidbots

Allows you to run simulations to see what the best item sets you can take on fights.

Wipefest

Reports where people went wrong on mechanics in fights that eventually lead to a wipe.
