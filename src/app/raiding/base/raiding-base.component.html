  • Expectations

    General Expectations

    These expectations hold true for both the Mythic and Heroic teams. Be sure to read these and be sure that you are willing to meet these expectations each and every night.

    • Be online on time and able to spend 3 hours uninterrupted. The raid team will not wait for you.
    • Let the Raid Leader know if you cannot make the raid. Consistently leaving early or random AFKs will reduce the chance you’ll be asked to return. No call/no shows will not be invited later in the night.
    • Attendance is crucial to progressing a raid tier.
    • Raiders will get an understanding of each fight prior to the first encounter with a boss.
    • Don’t be impaired while we are progressing. Once a raid is on farm, or doing non-progression raiding, we don’t care, so long as it doesn't negatively affect the social aspect of the group. "On farm" means we can clear a raid in one night.
    • Raid Assistants will be picked by the Raid Leader to help them. They can promote as many as they need. Raid Assistants are priority shot callers.
    • All raiders will have WeakAuras, Exorsus Raid Tools, and DBM (or BigWigs).

    To become a full-time raider, meet all of the noted expectations listed above, the expectations for your specific team, and show up (on time) for two consecutive weeks while participating in a raid team.

    You can lose raider rank by "no call, no showing" for two consecutive weeks.

  • Mythic Team

    Mythic Team

    Although our requirements are guidelines, the closer you are to them, the more likely you will be picked to join the team. Being as we can only have 20 members in the team on a given night, be prepared to sit on the bench at first, but people come and go. We will likely have you trial for the mythic team in one of our heroic teams.

    Expectations

    • Understand that the team is more important than the individual.
    • Willing to die infrequently and takes the initiative to improve their gameplay without prodding.
    • Agree that mistakes are a learning experience.
    • Be willing to take receive help and admit mistakes. Everyone struggles with certain fights from time. We have very clear expectations, but we are also all very friendly and willing to help people in those cases.
    • You can miss up to 2 raid nights per month to maintain a guaranteed raid spot. If you have to miss a raid, you are expected to give notice at least one day before unless it's an emergency.
    • Be prepared to wipe a lot; it’s the nature of the beast.
    • Mythic raiders will clear a +15 keystone every week.
    • Retrieve all necessary materials from the guild bank prior to raid time.
    • Raiders may be benched for another spec/class temporarily.
    • There are no carries in a mythic raid, everyone must pull their weight.
    • Heroic raid teams will double as a testing ground for those who want to join the mythic team.
    • Certain WeakAuras may be used on a team wide basis and everyone will import them when needed.
    • Raiders should watch a fight that is being progressed from your specific class’s perspective if possible.

    • Level 85 neck

  • Loot / BoEs

    Loot / BoEs

    We want to be very clear and explicit when it comes to loot drops as it can be a point of contention. Our loot rules are broken up into two types: personal and BoEs

    Personal Loot

    All personal loot belongs to the raider who looted it. They are under no obligation to trade it to any member of the raid. Traded gear will follow these rules when it is being rolled on:

    1. Main Spec (main character)
    2. Main Spec (side-grade)
    3. Off Spec
    4. Alts

    Our gearing philosophy places a higher importance on new and current member's main character above alts.

    BoEs

    BoE’s that drop during raid night will be given to the Raid Leader and sold on the AH for guild bank revenue. However, any guild member may buy the item BoE from the guild bank at half price. You will be supplied with ALL flasks, food, potions, vantus runes, and repairs needed for raiding and M+. Raiders may pull consumables out of the bank everyday as needed. This rule applies to any Uranium raider in any Uranium raid.
